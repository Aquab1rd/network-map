Submit / Update Info

Use these forms to add new people, churches, or organizations (Nodes) and to define relationships between them (Edges). Submissions are reviewed before appearing on the public map.

Privacy & Approval
Only approved entries appear on the map. Please provide accurate info and include a contact email so we can verify details if needed.

Register a New Node

Submit a person, church, supporter, or partner organization. After approval, it will appear on the map.

Add a Connection (Relationship)

Link two existing nodes (e.g., Leads, Supports, or Partners). Use the exact IDs or names from the map/sheet.

Tips

  • Use decimal coordinates (e.g., 59.437, 24.753). If you don’t know them, paste a Google Maps link.
  • Keep IDs short and unique (e.g., A001).
  • Relationships use: leads, supports, partners (others default to “linked”).
  • If multiple people share the same city, a small difference in coordinates prevents overlapping pins.
